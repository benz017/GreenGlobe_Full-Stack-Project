Our Story

Although the story started in the beginning of the 20th Century with the increased usage of plastics and growing pollution in our lands, air and above, its only since the last few decades of it we started taking the criticality of it seriously. And with the '21st Century Breakdown', we could see people and communities rising for bringing earth back to greener and pollution free place to live.

We here come with an idea to promote an initiative towards a greener earth in order to make it a better place to live in.
